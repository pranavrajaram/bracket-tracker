📋 Import from ESPN Bracket

1
Go to espn.com/tournament-challenge-bracket and open your bracket.
2
Click the Print Bracket button near the top of your bracket page. This opens a print-friendly page that shows all rounds — including the Final Four and Championship — which the regular view hides in dropdowns.
3
Once the print page is open, press Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows) to open the print preview, then click anywhere inside the preview window to focus it.
4
Press Cmd+A / Ctrl+A to select all text, then Cmd+C / Ctrl+C to copy.
5
Switch to the Paste tab above, paste the text, and click Parse Bracket.
⚠️ Why the print view? The regular ESPN bracket page collapses the Final Four and Championship sections into dropdowns. Selecting all on that page misses those picks. The print view renders every round flat, so all 63 picks are captured.
